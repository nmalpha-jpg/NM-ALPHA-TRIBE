The others largely do not.

Venice processed 45 billion LLM tokens per day by February 17 — a figure that doubled in just two weeks from the ~22B reported on February 4. Every token processed is real inference demand. SUPPLY SHOCK
💰
3. The Market Discovered the Revenue
In a sea of crypto-AI tokens built on narrative and vaporware, Venice has an actual business with actual users paying actual money. When a revenue-generating project with deflationary mechanics starts getting institutional attention, the re-rating is fast. Erik Jan 1, 2026 X post
REPRICING EVENT
🔒
4. Staking Locked Up the Float
Over 70% of circulating supply (~31M VVV) is staked earning ~18–19% APY. Another ~8M is locked for DIEM minting. Liquid supply is extraordinarily thin. When buy pressure hits a thin float, prices move exponentially — not linearly. The staking rate is a feature, not a coincidence. On-chain · venice.ai/token dashboard
THIN FLOAT DYNAMIC
